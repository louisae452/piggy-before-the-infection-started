Piggy before the infection started.

Piggy Before The Infection Started is SuperJakeJoseCat's unofficial prequel to the Piggy storyline, taking place before the infection. Piggy Before The Infection Started tells the story of Penny Piggy, a teenager living in Lucella with her friends, including Foxy who is always getting into fights with the school bully, Billy. At the same time, Piggy Before The Infection Started also tells the story of the Lucella Police Department and their journey to defeat dangerous criminal P199y.
